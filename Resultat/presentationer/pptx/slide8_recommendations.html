Recommendations

Key Takeaways

Target 80% Baseload

Best balance between delivery guarantee and capital cost. 8 MWh battery vs 109 MWh for 100%.

Negotiate Pass Hours

10% pass allowance reduces battery by 50-100%. Focus on summer night flexibility.

1:1 Solar/Wind Ratio

Optimal complementarity for SE3. Solar covers days, wind covers nights/winter.
