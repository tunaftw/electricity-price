Key Insight: Summer Nights Are the Bottleneck

Counter-Intuitive Finding

The most challenging periods are summer nights (July-August), NOT winter darkness!

Why Summer Nights?

  • Weaker summer winds - reduced pressure gradients in stable summer weather
  • Anticyclones persist - high pressure systems can stall for days
  • Solar = zero at night - regardless of season
  • Dunkelflaute - extended periods of low wind + no sun

Critical Periods Identified

  • 50% baseload: July 23-24 (16 hours)
  • 80% baseload: Nov 6-9 (69 hours)
  • 100% baseload: Multiple weeks
