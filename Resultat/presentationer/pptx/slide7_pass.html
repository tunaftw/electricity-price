Key Insight: 10% Pass Reduces Battery 50-100%

Effect of Allowing Non-Delivery Hours

If the PPA allows 10% non-delivery (878 hours/year), battery requirements drop dramatically:

Baseload Level 100% Delivery 90% Delivery Battery Reduction
50% (0.208 MW) 1.2 MWh 0 MWh -100%
80% (0.332 MW) 8.2 MWh 3.3 MWh -60%
100% (0.415 MW) 109 MWh 57 MWh -48%

Implication: The worst 10% of hours drive most of the battery requirement. Small contractual flexibility yields outsized savings.

Recommendation: Negotiate for pass hours in PPA contracts - even small allowances save significant capital.
