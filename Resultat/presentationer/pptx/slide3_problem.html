The Challenge: Baseload PPA

What is Baseload PPA?

A Power Purchase Agreement that guarantees constant power delivery 24/7/365, regardless of weather conditions.

Unlike pay-as-produced contracts, the seller bears all volume risk.

The Problem

  • Solar only produces during daytime
  • Wind varies unpredictably
  • Combined output still fluctuates significantly
  • Gaps must be filled somehow

Analysis Question

How large a battery is needed to guarantee baseload delivery from solar + wind?

Our Approach

  • 1 MW solar (PVsyst profile)
  • 1 MW wind (ENTSO-E actual data)
  • Hourly simulation for full year
  • Calculate max cumulative deficit
  • Test different baseload levels
